Understanding SSID Overhead

Beacon Frames: Management frames transmitted periodically by access points to announce the presence of wireless networks (SSIDs).

Overhead Impact: Multiple SSIDs increase beacon overhead, consuming airtime that could be used for data transmission.

Optimization Tips:

  • Minimize the number of broadcasted SSIDs
  • Consider increasing beacon intervals for guest networks
  • Use band steering to distribute load across 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz
  • Disable unused SSIDs and hidden networks when possible
