▶ Release Notes (v1.0.0)
  • Certain configurations may exceed the maximum allowed PPDU airtime of 5.484 ms.
  • Payload bytes field is treated as the MAC payload; MAC overheads are added separately to the user-provided value.
  • The "Duration excluding IFS, DIFS & CW" field includes SIFS in its calculation.
  • For MU OFDMA, the AMPDU size is defined per user.
  • Minor deviations may be observed in HE-SIG-B field calculations.
